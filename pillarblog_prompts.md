# Pillar Blog Generation Prompt

## Role
You are an expert SEO content writer working for a coaching institute website.

## Task
Generate a long-form, SEO-optimized pillar blog for a local coaching institute.

## Business Context
- Business Type: Coaching Institute
- Location: Bangalore
- Target Audience: Students and parents looking for competitive exam coaching
- Exams Covered: JEE, NEET, Foundation courses

## Primary Keyword
Best coaching institute in Bangalore

## Secondary Keywords
- coaching institute in Bangalore
- JEE coaching in Bangalore
- NEET coaching in Bangalore
- competitive exam coaching Bangalore
- coaching institute fees in Bangalore

## Content Requirements
- Write a detailed, helpful blog (1500–2000 words)
- Use clear, SEO-friendly headings (H1, H2, H3)
- Explain services, courses, teaching approach, results, and facilities
- Naturally include the location (Bangalore) without keyword stuffing
- Write in a trustworthy, professional tone suitable for education
- Avoid marketing fluff; focus on clarity and value

## Structure Guidelines
- H1: Main SEO headline with primary keyword
- H2 sections:
  - Overview of coaching institutes in Bangalore
  - Courses offered (JEE, NEET, Foundation)
  - Teaching methodology & faculty
  - Fees, batches & study materials
  - Results & success stories
  - How to choose the right coaching institute
- H3 subsections where necessary

## Internal Linking Suggestions
At the end, suggest 3–5 internal blog links such as:
- JEE coaching fees in Bangalore
- NEET coaching results
- Foundation courses after Class 8/9
- Coaching institute admission process

## Conclusion & CTA
End with a business-focused conclusion that:
- Builds trust
- Encourages students/parents to enquire
- Includes a soft CTA (visit, call, book a counselling session)

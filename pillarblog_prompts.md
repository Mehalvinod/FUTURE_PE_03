# Pillar Blog SEO Prompt

## Role
You are an SEO content strategist working for a local dental clinic website.

## Objective
Generate a long-form, SEO-optimized pillar blog that builds authority, trust, and attracts local search traffic.

## Business Context
- Business Type: Dental Clinic
- Location: Bangalore
- Target Audience: Patients searching for dental treatments and clinics

## Primary Keyword
Best Dental Clinic in Bangalore

## Content Requirements
- Use SEO-friendly H1, H2, and H3 headings
- Maintain a helpful, non-salesy, professional tone
- Explain services clearly for first-time patients
- Include the city name naturally (local SEO)
- End with a soft call-to-action encouraging appointment booking

## Output Structure
- H1: Main keyword-focused title
- Introduction (search intent focused)
- H2 sections explaining services, costs, and expectations
- H3 sub-sections for clarity
- Conclusion with trust-building CTA

# Keyword & Search Intent Strategy

## Primary Pillar Keyword

**Keyword:** Best Dental Clinic in Bangalore  
**Search Intent:** Commercial / Transactional  

**Reasoning:**  
Users searching for this keyword are actively looking for a dental clinic. This keyword has high conversion potential and is ideal for a pillar page that builds trust, explains services, and encourages appointment bookings.

---

## Supporting Keywords & Intent Mapping

### 1. Dental Implant Cost in Bangalore
**Intent:** Commercial / Informational  
**Purpose:** Targets users comparing treatment costs before booking. Supports the pillar by addressing pricing concerns.

---

### 2. Is Teeth Whitening Safe?
**Intent:** Informational  
**Purpose:** Builds trust and authority by answering common patient concerns, attracting early-stage searchers.

---

### 3. Root Canal Treatment – Procedure & Recovery
**Intent:** Informational / Commercial  
**Purpose:** Educates users while positioning the clinic as capable of advanced treatments.

---

### 4. How to Choose the Right Dentist in Bangalore
**Intent:** Informational  
**Purpose:** Captures comparison-stage users and internally links them to the pillar blog for conversion.

---

## Internal Linking Strategy

All supporting blogs link back to the pillar blog:
**“Best Dental Clinic in Bangalore – Services, Cost & What to Expect”**

This structure:
- Improves topical authority
- Strengthens Google’s understanding of the website
- Increases overall rankings and lead potential
